## Overview

Orbit reframes the to-do list around *momentum* — what you touched last, what's
gone cold, and what deserves attention now. Instead of an ever-growing backlog,
you see a living view of your work.

## What I did

- Designed the core interaction model and visual language
- Built the front end in React with a custom keyboard layer
- Shipped a local-first sync engine so it works offline

> The hard part wasn't the features — it was deciding what to leave out.

## Outcome

Used daily by a small beta group, with a median session of under two minutes —
exactly the "in and out" feel I was aiming for.
