## Overview

Ledger turns messy freelance income into a clear monthly picture: what came in,
what's owed, and how much is safe to pay yourself.

## Highlights

- Import transactions from CSV in one drag
- Automatic set-aside calculations for taxes
- Everything stays on your machine — no accounts, no cloud

## Stack

Built with Svelte and a small Rust core for the number-crunching.
