For a long time I treated writing as something you do *after* you've figured
something out. The problem is that you rarely fully figure anything out — so I
never wrote.

## The shift

Now I write from the middle of things. Half-formed ideas, dead ends, the bug
that took a day. It turns out the messy middle is the useful part for everyone
else, too.

## A small rule

If I learned something this week that past-me would have wanted to know, it's
worth a post — even a short one.
